Core Services

Emergency Care

Our 24/7 emergency department is fully equipped to handle urgent medical situations with rapid, expert care. Our team is trained to manage everything from trauma to critical care emergencies.

Surgical Services

We offer a wide range of surgical procedures, including general, orthopedic, cardiovascular, and minimally invasive surgeries, performed by highly skilled surgeons using the latest medical technology.

Maternity and Newborn Care

Our maternity unit provides comprehensive care for expectant mothers, including prenatal, delivery, and postpartum services. We also offer neonatal care for newborns who may need specialized medical attention.

Pediatric Care

We are committed to delivering exceptional care for children, from newborns to adolescents. Our pediatric specialists provide routine checkups, vaccinations, and treatment for acute and chronic illnesses.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment options for heart conditions. Services include heart health screenings, non-invasive diagnostics, interventional cardiology, and rehabilitation.
